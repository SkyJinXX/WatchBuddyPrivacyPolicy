Privacy Policy for WatchBuddy

Last Updated: June 6, 2025

1. Introduction

WatchBuddy ("we," "our," or "us") is a Chrome extension that enhances your YouTube viewing experience by providing an AI-powered voice assistant. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • OpenAI API Key: You provide your own OpenAI API key, which is stored locally on your device.
  • Voice Recordings: When you click the microphone button, we temporarily record your voice to process your questions.
  • Manual Subtitles: If you upload subtitle files, they are stored locally on your device.

2.2 Information We Automatically Collect

  • YouTube Video Data: We access the current video's title, ID, playback position, and available subtitles.
  • Usage Preferences: Settings like enhanced voice mode are stored locally.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Functionality

  • Process your voice questions and provide AI-powered responses
  • Provide context-aware answers based on your current video position
  • Cache subtitles for improved performance
  • Maintain conversation history for better user experience

3.2 Data Processing

  • Your voice recordings are converted to text and sent to OpenAI along with video context
  • We combine your questions with relevant video subtitles to provide accurate responses
  • Voice recordings are processed temporarily and not permanently stored

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Your OpenAI API key is stored securely in your browser's local storage
  • Subtitle data and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Conversation history is maintained locally with automatic cleanup (7 days)
  • Manual subtitles are stored locally with automatic cleanup (30 days)

4.2 Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Your questions and video context are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Subtitle Services: We may access subtitle download services (like downsub.com) to retrieve video subtitles.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • To OpenAI for AI processing (as described above)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible through your browser
  • Data Deletion: You can clear all extension data through Chrome's extension settings
  • API Key Management: You can update or remove your OpenAI API key at any time
  • Voice Recording: Voice recording only occurs when you actively click the microphone button

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page for WatchBuddy.

10. Permissions Explanation

10.1 Required Permissions

  • activeTab: To access YouTube video pages and extract video information
  • storage: To securely store your API key and preferences locally

10.2 Host Permissions

  • youtube.com: To access video content and metadata
  • api.openai.com: To send requests to OpenAI's API
  • Subtitle services: To download video subtitles when needed

This privacy policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of the WatchBuddy Chrome extension.
